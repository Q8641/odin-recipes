Spaghetti

Description

Simplicity meets sophistication in our Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio. This timeless Italian dish features al dente pasta tossed in a luscious garlic-infused olive oil sauce, sprinkled with red pepper flakes and fresh parsley. Elevate your dining experience with this effortless yet flavorful masterpiece.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the Spaghetti :
    Boil the spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Reserve a cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. Infuse the Olive Oil :
    In a large skillet over medium heat, warm the olive oil. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes, saut√©ing until the garlic is golden but not browned. Adjust the heat to prevent burning.
  3. Toss the Pasta :
    Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to coat each strand in the garlic-infused oil. If needed, add a bit of the reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.
  4. Season to Perfection :
    Season the pasta with salt and black pepper to taste. Continue tossing until the spaghetti is well-coated and infused with the flavors of garlic and red pepper.
  5. Garnish and Serve :
    Transfer the spaghetti to serving plates, garnishing with fresh chopped parsley. Finish with a generous sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese for an extra layer of savory goodness.
  6. Enjoy the Simplicity!
    Serve immediately and savor the simplicity of this Classic Spaghetti Aglio e Olio. Pair it with a crisp white wine, and revel in the delightful combination of garlic, olive oil, and a hint of heat. Mangia bene!
