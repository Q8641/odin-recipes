Sweet Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken with Vegetables

Description

Elevate your weeknight dinner with the vibrant flavors of our Sheet Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken with Vegetables. Juicy chicken thighs marinated in zesty lemon and garlic meet a colorful medley of roasted vegetables for a fuss-free, one-pan culinary delight. Effortless to prepare and a joy to devour, this recipe is a testament to the beauty of simplicity.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup baby carrots
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven :
    Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C) and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Marinate the Chicken :
    In a bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and black pepper. Coat the chicken thighs with this marinade, ensuring they are well-covered. Allow them to marinate for at least 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare the Vegetables :
    the halved baby potatoes, baby carrots, and broccoli florets on the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Arrange on the Sheet Pan :
    Arrange the marinated chicken thighs on the baking sheet among the vegetables. Place lemon slices over the chicken for an extra burst of flavor.
  5. Roast to Perfection :
    Roast in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. For a golden finish, broil for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  6. Garnish and Serve :
    Remove from the oven and sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of color. Serve the Sheet Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken with Vegetables straight from the pan, and enjoy the harmony of flavors and textures.
  7. Enjoy the dish !
    Experience the joy of a delicious and straightforward meal with minimal cleanup. This sheet pan wonder is perfect for busy nights, leaving you with more time to savor the delightful moments around the table. Bon appétit!
